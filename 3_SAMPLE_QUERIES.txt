Good report queries

Instead of

Tell me everything about Apple.

Use

Generate a financial report summarizing Apple's revenue, profitability, liquidity, management discussion, and risk factors for fiscal year 2020.

or

Summarize Apple's annual report for fiscal year 2020.

or

Generate an SEC filing analysis for Apple's 2020 Form 10-K.

Good comparison queries

Your architecture also naturally supports things like:

Compare Apple's business risks between 2020 and 2022.
Compare management's discussion of Services revenue between 2021 and 2023.
Compare Apple's capital allocation strategy between 2019 and 2023.
How did Apple's discussion of AI evolve between 2022 and 2024?
Compare Apple's R&D disclosures across the last three annual reports.

These fit SEC filings very well because they compare what management disclosed over time.
